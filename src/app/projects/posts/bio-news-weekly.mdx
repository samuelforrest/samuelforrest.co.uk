## Project Overview

Bio News Weekly is a digital biology newspaper platform designed to make scientific news accessible and engaging for students and enthusiasts.

## Key Features

### Content Management

Built a custom content management system that allows:

- Easy article publishing and editing
- Category organization
- Search functionality
- Featured articles section

### Design Philosophy

The design focuses on:

- **Readability**: Clean typography and ample whitespace
- **Responsiveness**: Seamless experience across all devices
- **Performance**: Fast loading times and optimized images
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 compliant

## Technical Stack

- **Framework**: Next.js 14
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Content**: MDX for article rendering
- **Deployment**: Vercel

## User Experience

The platform provides:

- Intuitive navigation
- Category-based browsing
- Mobile-first design
- Fast page transitions

## Performance Metrics

- Lighthouse score: 95+
- First Contentful Paint: < 1.2s
- Time to Interactive: < 2.0s
