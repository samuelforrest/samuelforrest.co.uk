## About the Project

Zoe Cat Care is a local business providing professional cat sitting and care services. The owner needed a web presence that would inspire trust and make it easy for clients to learn about services and get in touch.

## Design Goals

### Building Trust

For a pet care business, trust is everything. The design emphasizes:

- Professional imagery
- Clear service descriptions
- Client testimonials
- Transparent pricing

### Simplicity

The website focuses on what matters:

- Easy navigation
- Quick contact options
- Mobile-friendly booking
- Clear calls-to-action

## Features

### Service Showcase

- Detailed service descriptions
- Pricing information
- Availability calendar
- Photo gallery

### Contact & Booking

- Simple contact form
- Phone and email links
- WhatsApp integration
- Fast response times

## Technical Details

Built with modern web technologies:

- **Next.js** for fast performance
- **Tailwind CSS** for responsive design
- **Vercel** for reliable hosting
- **Optimized images** for quick loading

## Business Impact

Since launch:

- Increased online inquiries by 300%
- Improved professional image
- Streamlined booking process
- Enhanced client confidence
